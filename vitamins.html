Types Of Vitamins

Vitamins are essential nutrients that are divided into two main categories: fat-soluble vitamins and water-soluble vitamins. Here's a breakdown of the different types of vitamins within each category:

Fat-Soluble Vitamins:
1. Vitamin A (Retinol)
2. Vitamin D
3. Vitamin E
4. Vitamin K


Water-Soluble Vitamins:
1. Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid)
2. B-complex Vitamins:
> Vitamin B1 (Thiamine)
> Vitamin B2 (Riboflavin)
> Vitamin B3 (Niacin)
> Vitamin B5 (Pantothenic Acid)
> Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine)
> Vitamin B7 (Biotin)
> Vitamin B9 (Folate or Folic Acid)
> Vitamin B12 (Cyanocobalamin)

Vitamin Deficiencies

Deficiencies in essential vitamins can lead to various health issues. Here's a brief overview of the types of deficiencies and their associated consequences:
Vitamin A Deficiency: Night blindness, weakened immune system, skin problems, and impaired growth and development.
Vitamin D Deficiency: Weak bones, increased risk of fractures, compromised immune function, and potential mood disturbances.
Vitamin E Deficiency: Nerve and muscle damage, impaired immune response, skin issues, and vision problems.
Vitamin K Deficiency: Impaired blood clotting, excessive bleeding, and potential impact on bone health.
Vitamin C Deficiency (Scurvy): Fatigue, bleeding gums, slow wound healing, weakened immunity, and joint pain.
B-complex Vitamin Deficiencies: B1 Deficiency (Thiamine Deficiency): Beriberi - muscle wasting, nerve damage.
B2 Deficiency (Riboflavin Deficiency): Skin disorders, sore throat, mouth inflammation.
B3 Deficiency (Niacin Deficiency): Pellagra - skin lesions, digestive and neurological symptoms.
B5 Deficiency (Pantothenic Acid Deficiency): Fatigue, insomnia, digestive disturbances.
B6 Deficiency (Pyridoxine Deficiency): Neurological symptoms, mood issues.
B7 Deficiency (Biotin Deficiency): Rare - skin and hair problems, neurological symptoms.
B9 Deficiency (Folate Deficiency): Anemia, birth defects, cognitive impact.
B12 Deficiency (Cyanocobalamin Deficiency): Anemia, nerve damage, memory and cognitive issues.
